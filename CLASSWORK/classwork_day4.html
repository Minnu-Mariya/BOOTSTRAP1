Question 1:How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team by clicking the “Contact Us” button on the support page. We’re available via email (support@company.com ), live chat, and phone (1800-123-456) from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
Question 2: Where can I track my order or service request?
Go to the “Track Order” section on the support page and enter your order ID or request number. You’ll see the current status, estimated delivery time, and any recent updates.
Question 3: What should I do if I can’t log in to my account?
Click “Forgot Password?” on the login page, then follow the instructions to reset your password. If you still can’t access your account, contact support with your registered email ID for assistance.
